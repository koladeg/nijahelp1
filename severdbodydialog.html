Severd Body Parts (Avulsion)

Treatment

  • Wrap the detached part of the body in something clean
    send it to the hospital with the victim so that it may be reattached if possible
  • Stop the bleeding immediately.
  • Treat for shock if necessary. If breathing problems are present, begin rescue breathing techniques
  • If wound is not deep or is not bleeding severely,
    1. Gently cleanse with mild soap and warm water.
    2. Cover with a sterile dressing or clean cloth and bandage.
  • Get professional medical help immediately.
