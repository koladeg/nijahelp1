Sunstroke

Treatment

sunstroke is a serious medical condition when the body's temperature rises too high as a result of excessive heat exposure. The body loses its ability to cool itself and overheats.

  • Get professional medical help immediately.
  • Lower body temperature quickly by placing victim in partially filled tub of cool, not cold, water
    Briskly sponge victim's body until temperature is reduced; then towel dry.
  • Or wrap victim in cold, wet sheets in a well-ventilated room that has a fan or an air conditioner until body temperature is reduced
  • DO NOT give stimulating beverages, such as coffee, tea, or soda
