SPLINTERS

Slender Pieces of Wood Glass or Metal Objects that Lodge In or Under Skin

Treatment

  • Wash affected area with mild soap and water.
  • Sterilize a needle or tweezers by boiling for 10 minutes
  • Loosen skin around splinter with needle and use tweezers to remove splinter.
    If splinter breaks, get medical assistance
  • Dress/ bandage the wound:
    Change dressing daily
