Poisoning

Treatment

  • Seek for medical assistance immediately

  • DO NOT give any other first aid if victim is unconscious or is having convulsions
    1. Begin rescue breathing techniques or CPR if necessary.
    2. If victim is convulsing, protect from further injury; loosen tight clothing if possible.
  • If professional medical help cannot be reached immediately:
    1. DO NOT induce vomiting if poison is unknown, a corrosive substance(i.e., acid, cleaning fluid, lye, drain cleaner),or a petroleum product(i.e., gasoline, turpentine,paint thinner, lighter fluid). DO NOT use activated charcoal
    2. Induce vomiting if poison is known and is not a corrosive substance or petroleum product
  • Take poison container (or vomitus if poison is unknown) with victim to the hospital.
