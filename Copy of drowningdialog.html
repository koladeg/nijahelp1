Drowning

Treatment

  • Get Help:
    Notify a lifeguard or seek medical assistance
  • Check for Breathing:
    Place ear next to the person's mouth and nose to check for breathing
  • If the Person is Not Breathing:
    Check pulse for 10 seconds
  • If There is No Pulse, Start CPR
    For an adult or child above 5 years old:
    • place the heel of one hand on the center of the chest at the nipple line.
    • You can also push with one hand on top of the other.
    • Press down about 2 inches and make sure not to press on ribs
    • Do 30 chest compressions, at the rate of 100 per minute or more
      and let the chest rise completely between pushes.
      •
  • Repeat if person Is still not breathing
